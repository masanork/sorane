## What license applies to the transit stops data?

The dataset is published under [CC-BY-4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/). Attribution is required when reusing the CSV.

## Where can I download the file?

Use the **Stops CSV** distribution on the [Transit Stops](transit-stops.html) page, or fetch `static/stops.csv` from the built site.

## Is this real transit data?

No. This repository is a minimal sorane-okf/0.3 example for documentation and CI only.
